SINGLE COLORED

Just put your image size after our URL and you'll get a placeholder.
For example:
tkirplaceholder.org/360x120/18bc9c
tkirplaceholder.org/image?size=360x120&background=18bc9c

Set text color the third argument
tkirplaceholder.org/360x120/8a2be2/deb887
tkirplaceholder.org/image?size=360x120&background=8a2be2&color=deb887
